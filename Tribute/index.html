Beverley Bass

The First Female American Captain


Timeline of Beverley Bass' Life

  • 1952

    Born in Ft. Meyers, Florida

  • 1970

    Graduated high school and began college at Texas Christian University

  • 1971

    Began taking flight lessons at Forth Worth's Meacham Airport

  • 1973

    Got her first paid pilot job, at $5/hour, flying dead bodies for a mortician

  • 1974

    Received her bachelor's degree in Spanish and Interior Design from TCU

  • 1976

    Was the third female pilot hired by American Airlines as a flight engineer

  • 1986

    Became the first female captain of American Airlines in October and was able to lead the first all-female crew in the history of aviation in December

  • 1988

    Became a Captain Check Airman (CKA)

  • 1990

    Married Tom Stawicki, the overseer of the pilot pension fund for American Airlines

  • 1990

    Was on maternity leave for one day before being offered a job in flight standards, which she gladly accepted

  • 1991

    Gave birth to her son, Taylor Stawicki, returning to her position as CKA when he was only 9 weeks old

  • 1992

    Gave birth to her daughter, Paige Stawicki

  • 1994

    American Airlines chose Bass as one of their "Triple Seven Instructors" for the brand new Boeing 777, becoming the first women to captain one in an airline operation

  • 2001

    As a result of the 9/11 attacks on the Twin Towers, Bass' flight from Paris (CDG) to Dallas (DFW) was diverted to Gander, Newfoundland as a part of Operation Yellow Ribbon

  • 2008

    Retired from flying for American Airlines

  • 2011

    Returned to Gander for the town's commemoration ceremony on the 10-year anniversary of 9/11 and was interviewed by couple of writers

  • 2015

    Was invited to the opening night of Come From Away, a musical written by the couple who had interviewed her, Irene Sankoff and David Hein, focused on Gander in the days following the 9/11 attacks. Was able to meet and befriend Jenn Colella, the women who plays her in the musical

  • 2016

    At the closing night at the Seattle Repertory Theater, Bass presented Colella with her jacket, wings and service pin she was wearing on 9/11 so that they could be worn in the Broadway production

  • 2017

    Went on a four-day publicity run with Colella before Come From Away officially opened on Broadway

  • Present

    Has seen Come From Away over 70 times, frequently traveling to see the show using her free flight perks
